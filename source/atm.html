Atmospheric Properties at Altitude



Important Note:The main features of the ISA are the standard sea level values and the variation of temperature with altitude. The air is assumed as dry perfect gas.
The standard sea level conditions are as follows:
Temperature (T°) = 288.15 K = 150C
Pressure (P°) = 101325 N/m2 = 760 mm of Hg

Following values / expressions have been used while preparing
ISA table. R=287.05287 and g= 9.80665m/s.
